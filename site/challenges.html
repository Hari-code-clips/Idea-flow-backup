Arth Shakti Economy · Enterprise · Trade

"The power of prosperity."
VUP-AS-001

AI Kisan Salahkar — Farmer Advisor

Build a chatbot or web app where a UP farmer describes their crop type, soil conditions, land size, and weather in plain Hindi or English and receives personalised, actionable advice on crops, pest control, irrigation, and market prices, along with scheme eligibility.

World Bank $325M UP-AGREES scheme and PM Kisan initiative support 1 million+ UP farmers needing accessible, personalised agri-guidance.

VUP-AS-002

UP Heritage & Tourism Explorer

Create a web application showcasing Uttar Pradesh's rich cultural heritage — historical sites (Ayodhya, Varanasi, Agra, Sarnath), One District One Product (ODOP) artisan goods, and regional cuisines — with an interactive map and a basic trip itinerary builder.

UP is home to three of India's top tourism destinations and 75 districts with unique ODOP products, making it a major driver of cultural economy.

VUP-AS-003

Startup UP — Idea Validator

Build an AI-powered tool where aspiring UP entrepreneurs describe their startup idea and instantly receive market fit analysis, matching government schemes (MYUVA, StartinUP, CoE), a viability scorecard, competitor mapping, and actionable next steps.

UP has 16,000+ DPIIT-registered startups and is the 4th largest startup ecosystem in India, with active support via MYUVA loans and StartinUP programmes.

Srijan Shakti Infrastructure · Energy · Manufacturing

"The power of creation."
VUP-SS-001

Expressway Logistics Optimiser

Develop a tool for SMEs and manufacturers along UP's expressway corridors to plan freight routes, estimate cost and time, and locate the nearest Integrated Manufacturing and Logistics Centre (IMLC) across Purvanchal, Ganga, Gorakhpur Link, and Bundelkhand corridors.

UP has 27 IMLCs planned across 26 districts and the Ganga Expressway spanning 594 km, creating a high-growth logistics corridor for small manufacturers.

Use this challenge
VUP-SS-002

Green Energy Calculator for UP Households

Build a calculator where a UP household inputs their location, roof area, electricity consumption, and budget to receive a personalised solar panel recommendation — with 5–10 year savings projections, PM Surya Ghar Yojana subsidy eligibility, and CO₂ impact estimate.

UP has 3,483 MW of solar capacity with 2 lakh+ homes already solarised, targeting 500 GW nationally by 2030 under PM Surya Ghar Yojana.

Jeevan Shakti People · Skilling · Governance

"The power of life."
VUP-JS-001

Smart Gram Panchayat Dashboard

Build an interactive, mobile-friendly dashboard visualising rural development metrics for a UP gram panchayat — tracking sanitation, education, health, road connectivity, and digital access against 2047 targets — with category filters and a comparison view of current state vs. goals.

98+ lakh citizen suggestions have been incorporated into the Viksit UP vision, highlighting demand for grassroots-level progress tracking across 75 districts.

Use this challenge
VUP-JS-002

Skill Match UP

Create a platform that matches UP youth (aged 14–35) with relevant UPSDM skill development courses. Users input education, interests, location, and career goals to get tailored course recommendations across 654 trades in 53 sectors, nearest ITI finder, and career pathway visualisation.

UPSDM covers 654 trades with 3,258 ITIs spread across all 75 districts of UP, serving a large base of youth seeking vocational skilling and employment.

Use this challenge
VUP-JS-003

Viksit UP Citizen Feedback Analyser

Build an AI-powered tool that analyses batches of citizen feedback entries — auto-categorising by theme and sub-theme, detecting sentiment, tagging by district, assessing urgency, and generating prioritised executive summary reports with key insights and action points for government officials.

Over 9.8 million (98 lakh) citizen suggestions were received for the Viksit UP vision, creating a large-scale unstructured text analysis challenge for policymakers.
